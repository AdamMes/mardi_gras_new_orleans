The Ritz Carlton New Orleans Hotel

Rich in history and personality, New Orleans is unlike any other US city. Honoring its spirit and elegance, The Ritz-Carlton, New Orleans invites visitors to its French Quarter retreat, set within a 1908 Beaux Arts building. Here, one can bask in the silence of the hotel’s beautiful courtyard, experience the mysticism of the city through the spa’s Voodoo Ritual or learn how to play the trumpet with in-house jazz musician Jeremy Davenport.

The Ritz-Carlton, New Orleans provides you with world-class event support and renowned service. We have a large inventory of equipment and a wealth of experience in helping our guests achieve the goals for their meetings with creativity and professionalism. Our offerings include but are not limited to: general lighting, video, communications, audio, office equipment and meeting accessories.

The experience at The Ritz-Carlton Spa, New Orleans is about more than relaxation; it is way to connect with the city’s mystical spirit, charming warmth and defining essences. Signature treatments include a Voodoo Ritual and a couple’s treatment utilizing coffee and praline. Spread across 25,000 square feet, the spa is the largest in New Orleans and includes 22 treatment rooms, a café, and a boutique.

Our Ladies and Gentlemen are the key to the best our destination has to offer. Allow our carefully curated collection of local attractions and experiences help you customize the perfect vacation.
